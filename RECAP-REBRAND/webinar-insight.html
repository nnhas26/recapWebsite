INSIGHT SPEAKER SERIES WEBINAR presents a step-by-step approach to managing a business such as finance, marketing, HR, and technology. This webinar is specially tailored by presenting international experts as speakers who will discuss a wide range of topics and best practices for formulating business strategies to the current business environment in order to help startups for business decisions making and achieving their business goals. This webinar series will consist

of presentations from speakers and discussions that will be opened to the public but really meant for entrepreneurs, startups, professionals, and students. Webinar material will be delivered by international speakers for approximately 30 minutes followed by questions and answers for approximately 30 minutes through an online platform. This webinar will be guided by a Moderator who will ensure that the webinar is interactive and well delivered.

The objectives of this international webinar series are as follows:
  • Educate entrepreneurs and startups on how to manage a business in the midst of crisis due to COVID 19 pandemic
  • Increase business knowledge and awareness from the global perspective
  • Provide experience to startups to learn directly with international experts
This webinars have a wide range of topics around business and management including but not limited to, finance, marketing, and technology.
