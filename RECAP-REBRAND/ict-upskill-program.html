ICT UPSKILL PROGRAM is a program in collaboration with AITI which focuses on upgrading coding and programming skills for ICT Graduates. This program aims to develop competent and qualified Bruneian youth in order to become more suitable to be internationally recognized and employable.


In this program, the Upskill participants will undertake online courses, weekly Upskill classes with a trainer, and one-to-one sessions with allocated shadow mentors. As part of the program, the participants will have the opportunity to undertake an apprenticeship in Recap’s hiring partners for up to 3 months. Upon their completion, they are expected to be hired by the hiring companies.
